Overview (100 words) - This should be a summary of what the project will be.

The project I have chosen for this is designing an app/ website that allows users to rate parks for a variety of different categories. For example, rating parks on how well they are for running, suitable for dogs and relaxed events like picnics and group activities.

The drawbacks that this app will face is that a lot of the key areas (reviews and information on the parks) will require a lot of user input.

Description (500 words) - Detailed description of the features of the product or service Tools.

The features of the app will focus on a variety of ways users will be able to rate, suggest and community work with parks. These include:

  • A search feature that will be able to show users parks near them via searching by suburbs or using the map feature.
  • A way to either leave a review of the park while also being able to click on one of the icons that are a quick and easy way to show other users what the park is best for. e.g.
    • A dog on a lead to show that it is good for animals that will also show that this park allows animals
    • A picnic bench that will show it good for social events like a picnic or BBQ
    • A person running that will show that is good for people who would want to use the park for exercise
