What do the results of these tests mean for you?

These don’t mean much for me as it only shows a vague outline of the person because the answers, they give are very broad and don’t really apply to all events in a person’s day to day. Though it would assist to a degree on when working with team/ project members.

How do you think these results may influence your behaviour in a team?

I’m not sure these results would influence my behaviour in a team because I perform more on how the others in the team react around me and with the situations that occur during the team working together.

How should you take this into account when forming a team?

I would use this data at the start of forming a team, somewhat like a base/ outline on the selection process before I start taking a deeper look into the applicants. The reason why I would use this as a base/outline is because these personality tests only show a small part of the applicant’s skill/ abilities. Also, a bit problem with these test is the fact that a person sees themselves differently from how others see them and will provide those answers to cover up flaws.
